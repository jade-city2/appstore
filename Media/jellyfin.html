Jellyfin — Your Media, Your Way

Version: 11.6

A free and open source media player and entertainment hub for playing videos, music, pictures, and more.

 • 

Features

  • Plays all common media formats with hardware transcoding FREE
  • Add custom CSS and plugins to make it yours
  • Library management for movies, TV shows and music
  • Large selection of apps to play on your devices

Privacy & Notes

Jellyfin itself does not include content — you must supply your own media or install third-party add-ons. Review add-on privacy separately.
