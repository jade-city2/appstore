Kodi — Open Media Center

Version: 21.3

A free and open source media player and entertainment hub for playing videos, music, pictures, and more.

Official site  • 

Features

  • Plays all common media formats
  • Customizable with skins and add-ons
  • Library management for movies, TV shows and music
  • Remote control support and UPnP/DLNA streaming
